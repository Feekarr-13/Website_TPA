Principal's Speec
TPA AL-ISTIQOMAH is a Quran Education park located in Dk. Pekaleran, Sridadi Village, Sirampog District, Brebes Regency. TPA AL-ISTIQOMAH is an institution that plays a role in teaching the Quran to children from kindergarten to elementary school. The purpose of TPA AL-ISTIQOMAH is to create a generation of Muslims who are proficient and passionate about reading the Quran.
