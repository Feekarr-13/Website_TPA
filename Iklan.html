Principal's Speech
TPQ AL-MUKHLISIN is an Al-Quran Education Park which was founded in 1998 and is located in Dandangan Hamlet, Dlanggu Village, Deket District, Lamongan Regency. TPQ AL-MUKHLISIN is an institution that plays a role in teaching the Koran to children from kindergarten to elementary school. The aim of TPQ AL-MUKHLISIN is to create a generation of Muslims who are proficient and passionate about reading the Koran.
